About Zob Press

ZobPress Job Board Software let's you launch an easy-to-use advanced Job Board while providing all the key features to generate revenue and be successful in a competitive job board market. Jobbex is scalable and a fully customizable solution to meet your unique Job Board requirements.

Our ZobPress product - JobBoardSoftware, lets employers, recruiters and enterprenuers to publish their own Job Board that can be managed easily. Its development is based on great experience of developing and maintaining various job boards.
